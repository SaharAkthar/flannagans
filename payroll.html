Payroll Servise

Whether you’re in need of a dedicated payroll service or are looking for a firm to help with your payroll end of year returns, don’t hesitate to get in touch with our friendly team. We offer our wealth of expertise to business clients throughout the North East and beyond. Whatever your payroll requirements we have a package to suite your needs.

Our payroll service is accurate and quick. We understand how difficult it can be to keep up to date in employment related legislation and employment issues. We assure you a timely payroll service giving you more time to focus on important aspects of your business.

Payroll is an integral administrative but time consuming task for all businesses. And the task is made all the more difficult by the growing complexity of taxation and employment legislation and the accompanying regime of penalties for non-compliance. Our payroll services are fully compliant with Real Time Information (RTI) that releases valuable time for our clients to pursue the development of their businesses rather than being tied down with the red tape. So in order to support you we offer our comprehensive but confidential payroll services.

A member of our payroll specialists would be allocated to look after all of your payroll requirements, thereby giving you assurance of continuous support in your payroll.

Our payroll services include the following:

  • Processing payroll from standing and variable data provided
  • Dealing with statutory sick pay, maternity pay, paternity pay, attachment of earnings any other payroll addition or deduction required
  • Providing payslips or security payslips if necessary
  • Providing comprehensive reports including departmental analysis if required
  • Advising on PAYE liabilities
  • Completing annual returns
  • Providing advice on payroll and employment issues
  • Customised payslips (as required)
  • Summaries and analyses of staff costs
  • Administration of incentive schemes, bonuses, and ex-gratia and termination payments
